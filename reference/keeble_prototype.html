Reboot the Internet Router
network router internet
1
Locate the power cable
Find the black power cable plugged into the back of the router. It connects directly to the wall socket or power strip.
Photo: back of router
2
Unplug from the wall socket
Hold the plug head firmly and pull straight out of the socket. Don't tug the cable itself — only pull the plastic head.
3
Wait 30 seconds
This clears the router's memory completely. Use your phone's timer or count slowly to 30.
4
Plug back in & wait 2 minutes
Plug the cable back in. The router lights will blink. Wait until the internet light turns solid before testing your connection.
Photo: router lights
