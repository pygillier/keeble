# Viewing Documents

Keeble is designed to be easy to read on a mobile phone, even if you are not very familiar with technology.

## Reading a Guide

When you open a guide, you will see:

- A **title** at the top
- A list of **steps**, each in their own section
- **Images** where relevant (tap to view full screen)
- A **summary** or table at the end of longer guides

## Step-by-Step Sections

Long guides are split into numbered steps. Each step can be:

- **Expanded** — tap the step heading to show the full instructions
- **Collapsed** — tap again to hide them and keep the screen tidy

This lets you follow one step at a time without losing your place.

## Images

Photos and diagrams are displayed inline within each step.

- Tap any image to open it **full screen**.
- Pinch to zoom if you need to see a detail more clearly.
- Tap anywhere outside the image (or press the close button) to go back.

## Navigation

At the bottom of every page you will find the navigation bar:

| Icon | Page |
|---|---|
| Home | Return to the main page |
| Search | Search for a guide |
| Tags | Browse guides by category |
| Profile | Your account settings |
